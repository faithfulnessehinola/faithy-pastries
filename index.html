FAQ

At Faithy Pastries, we offer a wide variety of delicious treats. Our selection includes custom cakes for birthdays, weddings, and other celebrations, along with a range of cupcake flavors with creative toppings and fillings. We also bake fresh cookies, including chocolate chip and oatmeal varieties. Our pastry offerings include croissants, danishes, and puff pastries, as well as fruit tarts, cream pies, and other delectable treats. Additionally, we provide specialty desserts designed for your special events.

To ensure the best quality and timely delivery, we recommend placing regular orders at least 2-3 days in advance. For custom cakes and large orders, it's best to place your order 1-2 weeks ahead, depending on the complexity and size of the request. For weddings and large events, please contact us at least 1 month in advance to ensure everything is prepared to perfection. While we may be able to accommodate rush orders, we encourage early booking to avoid disappointment.

Yes, we offer delivery services for all our pastries. Delivery is available within designated areas, and we strive to ensure that your orders arrive fresh and on time. Delivery fees may apply depending on your location. For large or special event orders, we recommend booking in advance to secure your preferred delivery date and time.

Yes, at Faithy Pastries, we understand the importance of catering to dietary restrictions. We offer a selection of gluten-free and vegan pastries to accommodate different needs. Please inform us of any specific dietary requirements when placing your order, and we will do our best to create delicious treats that meet your needs.
